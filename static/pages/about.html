About

what is wapchan?

wapchan is an imageboard where users can anonymously post text and images.

what does the name mean?

wapchan was inspired by the term "wapanese", which is an old term that meant "white japanese", or someone obsessed with japanese culture. it's a fitting name for the theme of tis site. special thanks to this anon for the idea when i was tooling around with the idea back in october.
